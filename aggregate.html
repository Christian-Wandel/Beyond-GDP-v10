UN HLEG 2026

Headline Aggregate Indicators

The UN High-Level Expert Group on Beyond GDP recommends complementing the full indicator dashboard with a small number of headline aggregate indicators that summarise progress for communication and accountability — reported alongside GDP. This page implements the HLEG's two illustrative options for three focus countries with contrasting development contexts.

MVP — three illustrative countries. Denmark, Vietnam, and Kenya were selected to span high, lower-middle, and lower-income development contexts. Weights are editorial interpretations of cited national policy frameworks — not official government indices. The HLEG itself did not reach consensus on a single aggregation method.

Methodology: HLEG Option A = inequality-adjusted household income (GDP per capita proxy), cross-country comparable with fixed 2022 global-median Gini reference. HLEG Option B = multiplicative penalization of Option A score for underperformance on national-priority non-monetary dimensions. Weights sourced via expert-deliberation from national policy frameworks. Full methodology: _config/aggregate-methodology.md.
